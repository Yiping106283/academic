Yiping Ma


Welcome to my homepage! I am a PhD student in computer science at UPenn and I am broadly interested in security and privacy, cryptography, and computer systems. Much of my recent work focuses on private information retrieval and secure aggregation systems.


At UPenn, I am very fortunate to be advised by Sebastian Angel and Tal Rabin. I am also lucky to work with Yuval Ishai via visits to Technion, and Antigoni Polychroniadou during summer internship at JP Morgan AI Research. My research is supported by a Microsoft Research PhD Fellowship. Prior to PhD, I graduated with a Bachelar of Science from Turing Program at Peking University in 2020.


I will be starting as a postdoc at UC Berkeley working with Raluca Ada Popa.
